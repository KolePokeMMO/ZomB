ZomB Bounty Hunters

Bounty Hunter Style

Created by: XeroCTRL

Officer Jenny has recruited Team ZomB to assist in obtaining certain Pokémon. The rewards will be great and Officer Jenny is allowing whoever catches the target to become it's trainer permanently. Jails in the Pokéverse are overrun as it is and if a trainer is reliable enough to capture the target, then that trainer has proven themselves worthy of the task of keeping the target. Every member of Team ZomB has been officially hired as a Bounty Hunter. From the newest member to the mighty leaders of the team.

  • Prize
  • Location

1x Shiny Charm



Note: To claim the prize the shiny target catch must be linked in-game to an officer+.

Rules
  • The Shiny Pokémon must have your your own name as it's Original Trainer to claim the reward.
  • To claim the reward, the trainer who obtained the target must link it in team chat for an officer or above to see.
  • In the event two people catch the target- the winner will be decided by catch time.
  • The target must be caught either on or after being announced.
  • Can be from anywhere, as long as it's obtained after the target was announced.
  • Shinies owned prior to the announcement will not be considered.
  • A rebreed of a shiny already owned will not be accepted.
  • Only members of team ZomB can win the prize.
  • XeroCTRL will be mailing the rewards out. Do not bother other officers, Kelly or Kole about the reward.
